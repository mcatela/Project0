The cavaquinho is a small Portuguese string instrument in the European guitar family, with four wire or gut strings. More broadly, cavaquinho is the name of a four-stringed subdivision of the lute family of instruments. Due to its smal size, cavaquinho traveled around the world in the portuguese discoveries giving origin to several forms, each one used in different regions and for different styles of music.
